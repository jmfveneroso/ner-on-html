Staff in Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science has around 30 academic staff, a vibrant community of research assistants and doctoral students, a dedicated technical team, and strong administrative support. Contact details for all staff members in the Department of Computer Science are given below. Offices prefixed with B or D are located in the Warwick Maths Institute.

Head of Department - Professor Stephen Jarvis
Deputy Head of Department (Teaching) - Dr Abhir Bhalerao
Deputy Head of Department (Research) - Professor Rob Procter
Director of Undergraduate Studies - Dr. Matthew Leeke
Director of PGT Studies - Dr. Adam Chester
Head of Administration - Ruth Cooper
Departmental Secretary - Sharon Howard
